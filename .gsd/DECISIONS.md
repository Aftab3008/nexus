# Decisions Log

## Phase 1: Letters Feature

**Date**: 2026-02-11
**Status**: Discussion Complete

### Decisions

- **Rich Text**: Yes (Bold, Italic, lists, etc.)
- **Delivery**: Users can choose **Instant** or **Scheduled** delivery.
- **Notifications**: Push notifications are required upon delivery.
- **UI Metaphor**: Envelopes.
- **Micro-interactions**: "Dove" or similar flying animation on send.

### Constraints

- Animations must be performant (Lottie or Reanimated).
